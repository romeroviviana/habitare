Habitare International was founded by owner and entrepreneur, Carolina Pappe. Born in Venezuela, educated internationally, and with multi-national business experience, Ms. Pappe has a global perspective and experience that enables her to personally relate to and fluently communicate with diverse clients.

She takes pride in staying current with industry trends and conditions. As is clearly evidenced by the certifications she frequently adds to her long list of qualifications, she believes in constantly honing her professional skills in order to remain at the cutting edge in the services she and Habitare International provide.

During her many years in the real estate industry, Ms. Pappe noted the opportunity to build an Integrated Realty Service by establishing business liaisons with domestic and international companies and business executives. The result of these alliances is more than 100 years of business experience in the areas of Real Estate (purchase, sale, rental, staging, property management), Marketing, Finance, General Management , Renovations, Business Acquisitions, Money Exchange and Immigration Considerations for Investors working for its clients, both locally and internationally.

A company offering this magnitude of services under one umbrella is truly unique in the South Florida market and is a highly desirable option for both local clients as well as those from elsewhere in the United States or from abroad
